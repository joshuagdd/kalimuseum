Curator’s Note

The Ozeroan Archives is a tribute to the world within Legendary Kali — a fictional collection of portraits capturing those who defined a nation’s rise and fall. Each piece aims to preserve not just their likeness, but the fading essence of an era.

Artist’s Reflection

These portraits were crafted to reflect emotion over realism — evoking the fragility of memory and power. The muted palette and aged textures mirror the world’s decay, while the light on each subject symbolizes the fragments of hope left in Ozero’s history.

Curated & Designed by Joshua Gabrielle Domingo — 2025
